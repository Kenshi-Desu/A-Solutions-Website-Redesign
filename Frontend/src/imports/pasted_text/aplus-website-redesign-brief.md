Figma UI Design Brief: A+ Solutions Development Center (Website Redesign)

Project Overview: Redesign the public website for "A+ Solutions Development Center." The design must be modern, highly interactive, accessible, and structured to drive engagement.

CRITICAL AESTHETIC INSTRUCTION: The overarching theme must be a modern, bright white UI with a "Glassmorphism" aesthetic. Use the company's brand colors as subtle, blurry mesh gradients in the background, with frosted glass overlays for content containers.

1. Global Design System & Variables

Color Palette & Theming:

Base Theme (Background): Clean, bright white (#FFFFFF or #F8F9FA).

Background Effect: Create subtle, blurry mesh gradients (color orbs) in the background corners or behind main containers using the company's brand colors.

Overlays & Cards: Content cards and main containers should have a frosted glass effect (slight transparency with background blur, e.g., backdrop-filter: blur(12px)) so the colorful background orbs peek through subtly. Use soft drop shadows and rounded corners (12px or 16px).

Base Text Color: Dark Slate Gray (#333333) or Dark Blue (#2A3A53) for high contrast and readability.

Text Color Accent: Orange (#E37F4E) (Use for highlighted words, active tab states, and small eyebrow text).

UI Accents & Elements: Light Violet (#6F67BA) (Use for primary buttons, icon backgrounds, hover states, and graphic elements).

Typography (Google Font: Noto Sans):

H1 (Hero): 48px, Extra Bold (800), Line-height 120%, Letter-spacing -2%, Color: Dark Blue (with Orange accents).

H2 (Sections): 36px, Bold (700), Line-height 130%, Letter-spacing -1%, Color: Dark Blue.

H3 (Cards): 24px, Semi-Bold (600), Line-height 140%, Color: Dark Slate Gray.

Body (p): 16px, Regular (400), Line-height 160%, Color: Dark Slate Gray.

Global & Sitewide Improvements to Apply:

Navigation: Implement a fixed, sticky navigation bar on scroll with a frosted glass background. Tabs must be in this order: Home, OCRC, News & Updates, About Us, Contact Us.

Branding: Update favicon to match the logo. Use high-quality SVG/PNG assets.

Interactivity: Introduce modern CSS animations and micro-interactions (e.g., hover states on glass buttons/cards lifting up slightly).

Spacing: Standardize margins and padding to ensure content breathes properly with plenty of whitespace.

Footer: Expand to include comprehensive company info, quick links, and consistent branding. Include a tiny, minimal "lock" icon in the bottom right corner for Admin Login access.

2. Page Layout Instructions

Page 1: Home (Landing Page)

Hero Section: Replace plain text with a high-quality background video or dynamic slider (students in robotics/coding). Place a frosted glass container over the media to hold the H1 text and a strong CTA button in Light Violet ("Enroll Now").

Quick Achievements Banner (NEW): Right below the hero, add a horizontal glass banner with 3-4 quick stats/achievements (e.g., "10+ Years Experience," "500+ Students," "15+ Robotics Awards") using icons and Orange text accents.

Interactive Service Cards: Convert "Coding and Robotics Classes", "Innovation Projects", and "Academic Tutorials" into interactive glassmorphism cards with custom Light Violet icons, hover effects, and short text.

Social Proof: Add a section for parent/student testimonials using clean, modern card layouts.

Partners Showcase: Visually display high-quality logos of current partners/affiliated schools on a clean white background.

Page 2: OCRC (Olongapo City Robotics Cup)

Hero: Exciting event banner with H1 Title and a colorful mesh gradient background.

Timeline Section: Break up the heavy history paragraphs into a clean, visual CSS timeline showing the growth of OCRC over the years.

Media Gallery: Dynamic photo/video gallery showing past events with rounded corners.

Actionable Details: Clear section in a prominent glass card for Upcoming OCRC event (Date, Time, Venue, Eligibility, downloadable PDF rules).

CTAs & Authority: Prominent Light Violet buttons for "Register Your Team" and "Become a Sponsor." Highlight government/DepEd logos.

Page 3: News & Updates

Layout: A modern, grid-based or masonry layout to display recent posts.

Facebook Integration: Design custom UI cards that look native to the website (using the modern glass/white theme) but act as containers for content pulled dynamically from the official A+ Solutions Facebook page (https://www.facebook.com/APSDevCenter).

Design Note for UI: Each card should have placeholders for a prominent image, a truncated snippet of the Facebook post text, the date, and a "Read More on Facebook" link/button (Orange accent).

Page 4: About Us

Hero/Intro: Engaging headline with Orange accents on a clean, light background.

About A+ Solutions: A well-spaced section detailing the company's mission, vision, and core values using modern iconography.

Company Achievements & Awards (NEW): A dedicated section showcasing the company's major awards, recognitions, or certifications. Use a clean icon/image grid layout with the award title, year, and a small graphic/badge.

Our Team (Employees): A dedicated grid showcasing the instructors and staff using cohesive, high-quality headshots, their name (H3), role/title in Orange, and a brief bio inside white or frosted glass cards.

Page 5: Contact Us

Layout: 2-column grid.

Left Column (Info): Formatted, clickable links for the Phone and Email. Add Business Hours. Direct integration to Facebook Messenger.

Right Column (Form): Embedded, spam-protected contact form with modern input fields (light gray borders, rounded corners).

Map: Large, interactive Google Map embedded for the physical address with rounded borders (border-radius: 16px).

3. Image Utilization Strategy

Please use provided image files across the designs as follows to increase engagement:

Hero Banners: Dynamic, high-resolution images of students working on robotics or coding.

Backgrounds: Use subtle, bright mesh gradients or high-quality photos with a light/frosted glass overlay to ensure the dark text remains highly readable.

Cards/Galleries: Ensure images used in grids maintain consistent aspect ratios (e.g., 16:9 or 4:3) for a clean, uniform UI.
