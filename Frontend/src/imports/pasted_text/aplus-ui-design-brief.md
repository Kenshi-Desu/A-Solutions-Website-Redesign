Figma UI Design Brief: A+ Solutions Development Center

Project Overview: Redesign the website for "A+ Solutions Development Center." The design must be modern, highly interactive, accessible, and structured to drive engagement.

1. Global Design System & Variables

Color Palette:

Background (Primary): Dark Blue #2A3A53

Base Text Color: White #FFFFFF

Text Color Accent: Orange #E37F4E (Use for highlighted words, active tab states, and small eyebrow text).

UI Accents & Elements: Light Violet #6F67BA (Use for primary buttons, service card backgrounds, hover states, and graphic elements).

Typography (Google Font: Noto Sans):

H1 (Hero): 48px, Extra Bold (800), Line-height 120%, Letter-spacing -2%, Color: White (with Orange accents).

H2 (Sections): 36px, Bold (700), Line-height 130%, Letter-spacing -1%, Color: White.

H3 (Cards): 24px, Semi-Bold (600), Line-height 140%, Color: White.

Body (p): 16px, Regular (400), Line-height 160%, Color: White.

Global & Sitewide Improvements to Apply:

Navigation: Implement a fixed, sticky navigation bar on scroll. Tabs include: Home, OCRC, About Us, News & Updates, Contact Us.

Branding: Update favicon to match the logo. Use high-quality SVG/PNG assets.

Interactivity: Introduce modern CSS animations and micro-interactions (e.g., hover states on buttons/cards).

Spacing: Standardize margins and padding to ensure content breathes properly.

Footer: Expand to include comprehensive company info, quick links, and consistent branding. Auto-update copyright year and remove WordPress theme tags.

Technical UI: Ensure 100% mobile responsiveness, check color contrast (a11y), and design for optimized image placements.

2. Page Layout Instructions

Page 1: Home (Landing Page)

Hero Section: Replace plain text with a high-quality background video or dynamic slider showing students in robotics/coding (use provided images of students actively engaged in building or programming). Add a strong CTA button in Light Violet ("Enroll Now").

Interactive Service Cards: Convert "Coding and Robotics Classes", "Innovation Projects", and "Academic Tutorials" into interactive cards with custom icons, hover effects, and short text.

Social Proof: Add a section for reviews from parents/students and showcase student awards (use images of students receiving awards or medals).

Partners Showcase: Visually display high-quality logos of current partners/affiliated schools instead of plain text.

Page 2: OCRC (Olongapo City Robotics Cup)

Hero: Exciting event banner with H1 Title (use high-energy images from past OCRC events).

Timeline Section: Break up the heavy history paragraphs into a clean, visual CSS timeline showing the growth of OCRC over the years.

Media Gallery: Dynamic photo/video gallery showing past events (e.g., Ayala Mall).

Actionable Details: Clear section for Upcoming OCRC event (Date, Time, Venue, Eligibility, downloadable PDF rules).

CTAs & Authority: Prominent Light Violet buttons for "Register Your Team" and "Become a Sponsor." Highlight government/DepEd logos for credibility.

Page 3: About Us

Hero/Intro: Engaging headline with Orange accents (use an image showing the A+ Solutions facility or a wide shot of a classroom).

About A+ Solutions: A well-spaced section detailing the company's mission, vision, and core values. Use split text/image layouts to avoid walls of text.

Our Team (Employees): A dedicated grid showcasing the instructors and staff. Use cohesive, high-quality headshots (e.g., circular frames or unified card designs). Include their name (H3), role/title in Orange, and a brief bio.

Page 4: News & Updates (NEW)

Layout: A modern, grid-based or masonry layout to display recent posts and news.

Facebook Integration: Design custom UI cards that look native to the website (using the Dark Blue/Light Violet palette) but act as containers for content pulled dynamically from the official A+ Solutions Facebook page (https://www.facebook.com/APSDevCenter).

Design Note for UI: Each card should have placeholders for a prominent image, a truncated snippet of the Facebook post text, the date, and a "Read More on Facebook" link/button (Orange accent).

Goal: Avoid the generic, clunky look of standard Facebook embed iframes; use CSS to style the feed to match the site's branding perfectly while keeping the content fresh.

Page 5: Contact Us

Layout: 2-column grid for better visual flow and margin usage.

Left Column (Info): Formatted, clickable links for the Phone (+63 917 832 6822) and Email. Add Business Hours (e.g., Mon-Fri: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM). Direct integration to Facebook Messenger and other social icons.

Right Column (Form): Embedded, spam-protected (reCAPTCHA) contact form with interactive input fields.

Map: Large, interactive Google Map embedded for the physical address.

3. Image Utilization Strategy (For Figma Prompt)

Please use the provided image files across the designs as follows to increase engagement:

Hero Banners: Use dynamic, high-resolution images of students working on robotics or coding.

Backgrounds: Use images with a dark overlay to ensure white text remains readable.

Cards/Galleries: Ensure images used in grids (like the OCRC gallery or News cards) maintain consistent aspect ratios (e.g., 16:9 or 4:3) for a clean UI.
